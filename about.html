About

As an individual who started playing video games on the Nintendo 64, waking up on Sunday mornings to spend all day battling my brothers on Super Smash, I have been in love with video games ever since.

I do not intend for this website to be extremely serious or as in-depth as a professional gaming website with analysis. This is a place for me to explore why video-games meant what they did for me, and why certain ones were more fun than others.

With that being said, I do hope that you look around and see my thoughts on what games YOU love, and I would enjoy nothing more than beginning a conversation with you about any differences of opinion.

Enjoy!
